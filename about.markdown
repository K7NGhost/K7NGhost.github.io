# About Me

Hi, my name is Kevin Argueta a computer science student currently studying at VCU. I have a passion for learning new topics in the fields of programming and cybersecurity. For about a few years now I have been practicing both cybersecurity and programming. I supplement my desire to learn in cybersecurity by doing challenges and rooms on Tryhackme and have participated in two Capture The Flag contests. On the other hand, college has been keeping my desire to program in check with classes like Data Structures and Algorithms and Computer Systems. In addition, I have been doing both at my current internship with the Virginia State police in digital forensics where I got the opportunity to work with some of the top of the line forensics software as well as create scripts for extracting data. Oh, and I also enjoy going to the gym :)

What I hope to accomplish with this site is to be able to share with others my knowledge and to keep a sort of journal that I can look back on and see my progress in the field of computer science.
